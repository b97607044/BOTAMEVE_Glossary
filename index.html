About this glossary

Bodily injustice takes many forms — it can emerge from intimate relationships, social manipulation, structural power, and the quiet violence of norms we are taught to accept without question. This vocabulary exists to help us name what we face: the mechanisms of control, the erosions of self, and the systems that tell certain bodies they do not belong.

Healing is not a straight line. It moves three steps forward and two steps back. That is why not every word here points toward light — some name the wound before naming its remedy. We offer these terms to sit with the full complexity of what reclaiming a body, and a life, actually demands.

關於這份詞彙表

身體不正義呈現著多種樣貌——它可能源自親密關係、社會操弄、權勢結構，或是那些我們被教導必須無條件接受的規範，這些都是無聲的暴力。這套詞彙的存在，是為了幫助我們為辨認自身遭遇。

療癒從來不是一條直線。它總是前進三步，又退後兩步。這也是為什麼，這裡並非每個字詞都指向光明——有些詞彙在給出解方之前，必須先為傷口命名。我們提供這些語彙，是為了與這份複雜同在。
